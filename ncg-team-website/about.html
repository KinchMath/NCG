About NCG

Since its inception in 2004 with the support of Science Foundation Ireland and centred in Maynooth University, the NCG, has become firmly established as a leading centre for research in the field of Geocomputation, applying computational methods to large spatial data sets from acquisition to analysis, modelling and visualisation.
